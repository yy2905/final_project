COVID-19 Background Source: Social Media
Introduction
COVID-19, formerly known as 2019-nCoV disease, is an infectious disease happened in Wuhan, capital city of Hubei province, P.R.China in December 2019. With the first case confirmed on December 1st, 2019, over 110,000 people have been infected among 114 countries and regions. Thousands of people are fighting Death in the hospital. The number of confirmed cases, deaths and affected countries or regions are expected to climb further in the coming weeks.
